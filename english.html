Eye Examination – Appointment
It is often necessary to administer eye drops during a thorough examination which enlarge the eye’s pupils. Please be aware that after this procedure, driving is not allowed for a recommended five hours afterwards.

Please always bring relevant test results and prescription with you.

When being fitted for glasses, contact lenses should not be worn for a minimum of 24hours before your appointment.
